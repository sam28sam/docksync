Step 07
Driver arrives.
QR check-in.
The driver arrives at your gatehouse and scans your unique QR code with their smartphone — no app required. They enter their name, vehicle registration, and booking token. The system matches the scan to their booking, confirms their bay, and logs the exact arrival time. Automatically.
Print your QR code on A4, laminate it, and display it at the gatehouse entrance. Drivers scan it with any smartphone camera — no app download needed.
