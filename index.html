FAQ

Questions before
you start?

01How long does it take to get started?
Under 5 minutes. Add your bays, set operating hours and booking rules, then share your supplier booking link. Most teams have their first live booking within an hour of signing up.
02Will my suppliers need training?
No. The supplier portal requires no account or login, and takes under 2 minutes to use. We'll send your suppliers a one-page onboarding email on your behalf if you'd like.
03Can I manage multiple warehouses?
Each warehouse runs as a separate account — which many operations managers prefer as it keeps each site focused. Enterprise customers with multiple sites get volume pricing. A unified multi-warehouse dashboard is on the roadmap.
04Is the 30-day trial truly free?
Yes. Full access, no payment details required, no automatic billing at the end. You decide when you're ready to subscribe — no pressure, no sales calls.
05Do I need IT support to set up DockSync?
No. DockSync runs entirely in your browser — Chrome, Edge, or Safari. Nothing to install or maintain. Your IT team doesn't need to be involved at all for the standard setup.
06How does the QR code driver check-in work?
From My Account → QR Code, download your unique code and display or print it at the gatehouse. Drivers scan it on arrival (no app needed). The system logs check-in time, confirms their bay, and updates your dashboard in real time.
