Pricing questions

Everything you
need to know

01 Is the 30-day trial truly free?
Yes. Full access to every feature on the Growth plan, no payment details required, no automatic billing at the end. When your trial ends your account is paused — not deleted — and you can reactivate it when you're ready. No pressure, no sales calls.
02 What happens when I upgrade from Starter to Growth?
Upgrades take effect immediately. You'll be charged a pro-rated amount for the remainder of your current billing period. Your bays, bookings, and supplier data all carry over — nothing is lost or reset.
03 Can I manage multiple warehouses?
Currently each warehouse runs as a separate account — which many operations managers prefer as it keeps each site focused and clean. Enterprise customers managing multiple sites get volume pricing. A unified multi-warehouse dashboard is on the roadmap. Contact us at hello@docksync.app to discuss multi-site requirements.
04 How does annual billing work?
Annual plans are billed once per year in advance at the discounted rate. Starter annual is £63/mo (billed as £756/year, saving £190 vs monthly). Growth annual is £143/mo (billed as £1,716/year, saving £430 vs monthly). You can switch between monthly and annual at any time from My Account → Subscription.
05 How do I cancel?
Cancel anytime from My Account → Subscription. No cancellation fees. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period. If you cancel during an annual plan, no partial refunds are issued — but you keep full access until the period ends.
06 Are there any setup fees or hidden costs?
No. No setup fees. No per-user fees. No add-on charges. The price you see is the price you pay, plus UK VAT. Enterprise plans are custom-priced and agreed upfront in writing with no surprises.
07 What payment methods do you accept?
All major credit and debit cards via Stripe (Visa, Mastercard, American Express). Enterprise customers can also pay by purchase order (PO) or bank transfer. All card payments are handled by Stripe, which is PCI DSS Level 1 certified — the highest payment security level available.
